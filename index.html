BAGAICHA BLISS ORIGIN

Experience the journey of coffee, crafted with care at every step.

ROOTED IN NUWAKOT

Nestled in the pristine hills of Nuwakot District, Northern Nepal, our coffee grows at elevations of 800m to 1600m. Beneath the shade of native trees and nurtured by a perfect mountain climate (15°C – 25°C), our organic coffee develops a pure, rich, and unmistakable mountain flavor.

From harvest to roast, every stage is handled with intention. Only the finest cherries are selected, processed with care, and roasted in precise conditions that unlock their unique, balanced profile.

CULTIVATED WITH CARE & PURPOSE

From sustainable farming to hand-picking and natural processing, every step honors the land and the people.

Step 01

ORGANIC & SUSTAINABLE

High in the misty hills of Nuwakot, our journey starts in carefully nurtured coffee gardens. Each plant is grown with patience, care, and respect for nature. Bathed in sunlight and cooled by mountain air, the cherries slowly ripen to their perfect shade of deep red. Not every cherry makes the cut. Our farmers handpick only the ripest, richest red cherries to ensure unmatched flavor and aroma. This careful selection preserves the sweetness and character of each bean.

Step 02

CAREFUL HAND-PICKING

After harvesting, the beans are carefully roasted in small batches. Roasting unlocks the unique personality of each bean — from bold and intense to smooth and chocolatey. Timing, temperature, and technique combine to create a balanced flavor profile. Each bean is hand-picked with the support of local farmers, ensuring only the finest, fully ripe cherries make the cut. We use traditional wet processing methods, followed by natural drying, to unlock their unique, balanced profile.

Step 03

EMPOWERING THE COMMUNITY

We don't just grow coffee; we grow communities. Bagaicha Bliss distributes coffee plants and runs campaign programs for dedicated farmers and students, supporting local livelihoods and preserving Nepal's rich coffee heritage. Through fair wages and training programs, we ensure every family involved in our supply chain thrives alongside the business, creating a cycle of prosperity that strengthens the entire region.

Step 04

A PREMIUM EXPERIENCE

Every cup of Bagaicha Bliss holds the bold, intense, yet smooth and chocolatey personality of its origin. It is a warm, comforting journey from the Nepalese soil directly to your daily ritual. Whether you enjoy it as a morning pour-over or an afternoon espresso, each sip carries the altitude, the terroir, and the dedication of an entire community committed to excellence.

PRESERVING HERITAGE

We are committed to preserving coffee culture and promoting single-origin coffee from the mountains of Nepal. Experience the emotion, quality, and origin in every sip.
